What to Pre-Size in Phase 1
Build these Phase 2 accommodations into Phase 1 at minimal added cost:
Service Panel
Install 200A panel (or upgrade) with dedicated breaker slots for Phase 2 inverter. Cost to do this in Phase 1 vs. Phase 2: saves ~$400 in labor.
Conduit Home-Run
Run empty 1.5" conduit from Phase 2 array location to inverter/battery location while trench is open. Cost to add now vs. re-dig later: saves ~$600–900.
Battery Bank Busbar
Install EG4 external busbar sized for 6 batteries, populate 4 now. Phase 2 adds 2 more — no rewiring needed.
Inverter Parallel Port
EG4 18kPV has a dedicated parallel communication port. Leave accessible. Phase 2 inverter plugs in directly.

Which Plan for You?
Choose Plan A if…
· You have the capital available now
· You want maximum immediate energy independence
· You want to minimize total long-term spend (~$1,500–2,500 savings vs phased)
· You prefer one permit, one contractor mobilization
Choose Plan B if…
· You prefer to spread capital outlay over time
· You want Phase 1 savings to help fund Phase 2
· You want to validate the system before committing fully
· Your current summer deficit bothers you less than a large upfront check
