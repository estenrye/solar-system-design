Xcel Energy Requirements

  • File Solar*Rewards application (system size ≤20kW DC)
  • Net metering interconnection agreement required
  • System must not exceed 120% of historical usage in ideal conditions
  • UL1741 SA certified inverters required (FlexBOSS21 ✓)
  • Anti-islanding protection in all inverters
  • Utility-visible lockable disconnect may be required
  • Production meter may be required (confirm with Xcel)
  • Inspection by Xcel before Permission to Operate (PTO)

NEC 2023 Key Requirements

  • NEC 690.12: Rapid shutdown required — all DC circuits >1.5V / 1A
  • NEC 690.15: DC source disconnect at each array
  • NEC 690.47: DC grounding — all array frames to EGC
  • NEC 250.24: Single N-G bond at GridBOSS (first disconnect)
  • NEC 705: Interconnected power production sources
  • NEC 706: Energy storage systems — battery installation
  • All conductors in conduit or approved raceway
  • Non-combustible mounting surface for all equipment

Installation Notes

  • GridBOSS: do not mount in direct sunlight — use shade cover or shade structure
  • FlexBOSS21: indoor or protected location preferred; keep below 104°F ambient
  • Batteries: indoor or IP65-rated outdoor location; EG4 PowerPro is all-weather rated
  • Parallel comm cable: max recommended length per EG4 documentation
  • Battery CAN cable: use EG4 supplied cables — do not substitute
  • DC wiring: route on front face of rafters only — never across panel rear face
  • All conduit runs: seal all penetrations through building envelope
  • Wire sizing: verify voltage drop does not exceed 2% on any DC run
